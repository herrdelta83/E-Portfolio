# Example Case Study

Replace this with a real write-up once a milestone project is done. Suggested
structure:

## Problem
What you set out to build and why it matters.

## Approach
Key technical decisions — and the ones you reconsidered.

## Result
What works today, with a link to the live demo or repo.

## What I'd do differently
Honest retro — this is often the most useful part for readers.
